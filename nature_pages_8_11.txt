Embedded capacity analysis
Embedding capacity is a metric that describes the number of bits of information that can be inserted into the 
cover image15. This scheme uses a color cover image of size 512 × 512 and a grayscale watermark image of 256 × 
256. Therefore, the embedding capacity of this scheme is (256 × 256 × 8)/(512 × 512 × 3) = 0.6667bpp, which 
has a high embedding capacity.
